About Me

The turmoil of 2020 led me to realize and appreciate how much technology affects our health, happiness, and everyday life. Technology gives us the capability to explore the world, express our creativity, challenge ourselves and connect with others all from our couch. As a software developer, I am eager to learn and collaborate with others to continue making the world a bigger and better place.

I have developed projects using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am constantly working on adding more languages and skills to my repertoire. I can contribute to your company using my front-end expertise as I continue to develop my full-stack knowledge.

During my time away from the computer, I really enjoy adventuring with my husband. Our adventures include:

  • Finding new trails to shred on our mountain bikes
  • Cruising around town on our OneWheels
  • Trying out new coffee shops and local restaurants
  • Camping and enjoying nature

...and watching plenty of Youtube and Netflix.
